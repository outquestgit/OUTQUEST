1. Introduction

OutQuest ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our platform at outquest.com.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly when you:

  • Submit a quest interest form (name, email, WhatsApp number)
  • Apply as a partner (business name, offering details, contact info)
  • Subscribe to the journal or newsletter

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to send you quest-related resources and updates, match you with relevant partner offerings, respond to your enquiries, and improve our platform and content.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share limited information with partners only when you have specifically expressed interest in their offering through a quest.

5. Cookies

We use cookies to understand how visitors interact with the site and to maintain session state. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though some features may not function correctly.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes outlined in this policy, or as required by law.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. To exercise these rights, contact us at legal@outquest.com.

8. Contact

For any privacy-related questions, please reach out to legal@outquest.com.
